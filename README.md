# What I couldn't see then. 
Hi, It's been a while old pal, hasn't it? I hope you don't mind, do you have one minute of your day to spare? I promise I'll be quick. I wouldn't ever want to drag you back to square one, disrupt your healing or anything of the sort, therefore, if by any chance you believe this isn't worth your time, please feel free to walk away, be it indefinitely or temporarily. Either way I'll understand. If you ever choose to come back, this will be here waiting for you, for a while that is. 

I'll cut to the chase then: you know I don't like burning bridges, I suck at it. I'm afraid of the ashes, the bitter smell of burning time and the thought of not treading the lands on the other side ever again. So, will you please hold this for me (hands large brick*) while I say some dumb shit. You need not attempt to cross the river below for I believe my piercing rant is audible enough. I'm sorry if these stones are to heave a load. It was never my intent to make you bear such an unbearable cross. 

Please believe me when I tell you I've come a long way. Innumerable are the nights I've dozed off staring at the ceiling as I counted all of my faults. I was inconsiderate, brash, rude, unforgiving, impatient, beastly, pedantic, selfish, a total bore, beyond dull, a know-it-all, yes, yes, please chime in, I know you must have a few more to add. Lay it on me, I deserve it. But I was in love nonetheless. There was something I felt I tried to give you. A childish love. But wait. Let me explain. I don't mean that in the traditional meaning of "Childish", I by no means wish to say it was something superficial, foolish and irresponsible, but rather, I look at childish (in this context), as a higher value of sorts. Something to be exalted for it's purity, fervor, it's inability to be defined. I'm certain that when you were younger, you must've fallen whole-heartedly for something as well. Something that made your heart skip a beat, something only a naïve child could fully understand. 

It took me a whole year to finally come to terms with a lot of things. They boiled beneath the surface for so long that it was only a matter of time until I had to rise. This isn't it though, I wouldn't dare lay unmeditated thoughts on you like this, and in the process waste your time. What I write here is the culmination of quite a few personal essays, long thought sessions and nightly walks. But once more, I don't find it fair at all to put you back on this cycle, or wavelength rather, since at the end of the day, we've traversed different paths to get here. You once asked why I did this to you? Kept pulling you back in. At the time I didn't really have an answer, so I wondered, did I like the sense of power? Was I a cold hearted bastard that wished even more pain for the hurting? Do believe I considered these dark thoughts. I sat with them. But I realized that what I did to you wasn't a product of malice, but rather of incompetence. 

There's so much I learned being by your side. It's a tragedy that time has passed too soon, yet I've become wiser too late. So please forgive my contradictions as I try to explain this, but there were two sides to our love in my eyes. The pure, kindled love, this so called "childish" (in the good sense) love and, on the other hand, the thoughtful, timely, considerate love. I lacked the second, and therefore could never give you what I wanted, I could never give you my highest self (but please don't judge me too harshly for this, for I've just now realized it). It took me distance, a step back, to realize what I was getting wrong, and believe me, I've still got a long way to go. Maybe there's things I'll only realize when I see my own kids fall in love for the very first time. So if your heart allows you to do so, please do forgive me. I'm haunted by how idiotically I treated you: I mocked what you liked, discarded your values, wouldn't let your soul speak, despite hearing it's murmurs as my heel pressed it's throat, I didn't give you liberty to express yourself thoroughly, not for you to act in accordance to my liking, on the contrary. In fact, I believe the process of the "true you" emerging would've brought about more friction, yet in hindsight, I would've preferred that. I prefer that fire, that incontrollable chaos, contrary to someone tame, someone I reduced to nothing, someone I wouldn't let be. 

Furthermore, I'm very sorry for my lack of commitment. Forgive me for not fucking dancing with you once. Not fucking once, I couldn't go out of my way as to give you just one dance? (Forgive my French, but my disappointment in my past self is quite considerable). I'm sorry for not stepping outside of my comfort zone for you. I'm sorry
